special effects animators..what do they do?

Create special effects or animations using film, video, computers, or other electronic tools and media for use in products, such as computer games, movies, music videos, and commercials.

On the job you would

Design complex graphics and animation, using independent judgment, creativity, and computer equipment. Create basic designs, drawings, and illustrations for product labels, cartons, direct mail, or television. Participate in design and production of multimedia campaigns, handling budgeting and scheduling, and assisting with such responsibilities as production coordination, background design, and progress tracking.
